# Oviya Studio — Phase 1 (Brand + Design System + Assets)

Phase 1 of the Final Build Superpass: rebrand, design system, brand/preset imagery, and the
premium UI primitives that Phases 2 and 3 build on. No change to the generation engine, fidelity
gate, credit ledger, RLS, upload validation, taxonomy, or draft persistence.

## What "Oviya" means
Oviya (Tamil ஓவியா) means "one who is artistic" / "a work of art." South Indian female name. The
brand sits at the intersection of modern Indian cultural richness and Western editorial fashion.
Positioning: **Every product, a work of art.** / *Your product. Your muse. Your shoot.*

## 1. Rebrand (user-facing only)
Every user-visible "Drape" is now "Oviya" (short) / "Oviya Studio" (full). Internal identifiers were
deliberately left untouched: the `drape_` DB namespace, the `drape-presets` storage bucket, the
`dd-drape` repo/deploy names, env vars (`DRAPE_VIDEO_ENABLED`, `GEMINI_API_KEY`), the
`com.drape.generation` C2PA assertion namespace, and the LLM director system-prompt personas
(`You are Drape's ...`, internal, never seen by users). Renaming any of those would break live
shared infra.

Surfaces rebranded: root + per-page metadata/titles, OpenGraph/Twitter cards, the app rail and auth
wordmarks, landing page copy, terms/privacy, the credits/contact mailto subject, the C2PA
`claim_generator` and `softwareAgent` embedded in downloaded image provenance, and the RED-tier
copy in `compose.ts` / `qc.ts`.

Studio vocabulary applied where it was the natural label: shots gallery → **Contact sheet**, saved
models → **Your muses** / **Casting board**, new shot → **On set**, "Generate" energy → "Shoot."
(The full workspace vocabulary lands in Phase 2.)

## 2. Design system (`src/app/globals.css`)
Dark cinematic editorial. Token names were preserved for continuity; values repointed.
- **Surfaces** (never pure black): base `#0B0B0F`, four elevation levels up to `#232330`.
- **Text** (never pure white): primary `#F1F5F9`, secondary `#E0E0E0`, muted `#8A8A93`.
- **One committed accent**: temple gold / champagne `#C9A24B` (`--accent`, with `--saffron` and
  `--orchid` aliased to it so the system is genuinely single-accent).
- **Type**: Fraunces (serif display) + Inter (grotesk body).
- **Glass** (`.glass`, overlays only), **film grain** (`.grain`, fixed SVG overlay at 0.09,
  hidden under reduced-motion), elevation shadows, and motion tokens.
- **Motion** (`src/lib/motion.ts`): spring stiffness 350 / damping 18, hover ≤1.04, tap 0.97,
  entrance ~400ms, ease cubic-bezier(0.16,1,0.3,1), transform+opacity only, reduced-motion aware.

## 3. Brand + preset imagery (Gemini, `scripts/generate-assets.ts`)
25 assets generated with Gemini Nano Banana Pro (`gemini-3-pro-image`, flash fallback). **Gemini is
used for brand/marketing/preset imagery only. The live product engine stays on fal.** Prompts carry
a hard no-text / no-magazine-framing constraint so we can overlay our own copy.
- 2 heroes (4:5 portrait, 16:9 banner)
- 6 gallery editorials (saree, lehenga, western, jewellery, street, accessory)
- 3 before/after pairs — the "after" is generated by feeding the flat-lay "before" back in as an
  image input, so the garment genuinely corresponds (verified: teal chikankari kurta transfers
  faithfully onto the model).
- 8 "Looks" / Editorial preset thumbnails (high-gloss, noir, heritage, catalog, campaign, couture,
  street, gala)
- 3 empty-state / sample-product assets

Files live under `public/{brand,gallery,before-after,looks,empty}` with `public/brand/manifest.json`
(id → path). Every prompt is documented in `docs/ASSET_PROMPTS.md`. The script is idempotent
(skips existing files; `FORCE=1` to regenerate).
